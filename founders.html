We invite you to join us.

The Founders Challenge is a three-year commitment. This isn’t simply a donation, it’s an investment in the success of other founders. Lasting support empowers us to plan for our future and move forward with confidence. It frees us from the constraints of limited thinking, allows us to be singularly focused on cultivating world class innovators capable of creating solutions to Africa's biggest problems; education, healthcare, even democracy!
