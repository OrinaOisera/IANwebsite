The Problem

85

Unemployed youth in Kenya between the age of 18-35

9,000,000

Youth expected to join labor force by 2025

The Solution

We are a non profit startup studio on a mission to ensure young talented Africans can access Africa's digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners.

"Having a front-row seat to Changing the African Narrative has completely shifted my mindset. It is no longer about simply advancing in my career; the work we do is bigger than us and this has helped me realize my true potential"
Damaris Kongin - Cohort III, 2020

1. Developing Innovation Leaders

We provide 12 Month Innovation Fellowships to talented college graduates allowing them to participate in Africa’s digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners.

My biggest milestone has been seeing a project transition into a startup. Working on Jenga School has been challenging yet fulfilling. I'm thrilled about what more we can do.
Raphael Waithaka - Jenga School

2. Launching Startups

We work on projects with the intent of spinning them out into viable early stage startups.

"My biggest milestone has been seeing a project transition into a startup. Working on Jenga School has been challenging yet fulfilling. I'm thrilled about what more we can do"
Raphael Waithaka - Cohort II, 2019

3. Building Community

We continue to build a network of enablers including mentors, donors, and advisors to help catalyze the work we do.
