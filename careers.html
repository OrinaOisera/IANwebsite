Impact Africa Network
Who we are:

Impact Africa Network is a startup studio in Nairobi Kenya, on a mission to ensure young talented Africans can access Africa’s digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners. .

What we do:

We provide 12 month Innovation Fellowships to talented college graduates offering them the opportunity to work on well-vetted ideas with like-minded peers under the guidance of an experienced leadership team and mentor network

Our Mission:

To ensure young talented Africans can access Africa’s digital transformation opportunity as creators and owner.

Our Approach:

  • Develop Innovation Leaders
  • Launch Startups
  • Build Community
